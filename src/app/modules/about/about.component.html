How I got into programming?


I was working on Sound Design and wanted to know more about a particular VST. So, I watched an interview with the creator, Steve Duda...

He talked about the way he created it, programmatically, and it really intrigued me. He spoke about it being years of work. I thought to be able to make something as fine-tuned as that was amazing!
